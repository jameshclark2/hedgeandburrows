Our guarantee

A lasting solution, not just a quick fix.

If pest activity continues after treatment, we'll return free of charge within the agreed treatment period to reassess and take whatever further action is needed. Since pests can move between neighbouring properties, we may also recommend nearby areas are checked or treated, to reduce the chance of the problem coming back. We'll always explain our reasoning clearly and work with you to find the most practical solution.
